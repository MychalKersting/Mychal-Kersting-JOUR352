"Sticky Stuff" and Spin Rates

Detailed Results of the Crackdown in 2021

By Mychal Kersting

Pitchers in Major League Baseball thought the league's change in the new balls was one that did not benefit them. Pitchers lost grip on the ball, hence making it harder for them to locate pitches properly.

Pitchers were accused sticky substances, such as pine tar, a rosin/sunscreen combonation, and most infamously, spider-tack, to get control back. It worked, as pitchers became more dominant in a very short period of time. The league decided to crack down on all sticky substances in June of 2021.
